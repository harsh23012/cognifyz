Guess My Number

Game Rules:

1. The computer will think of a number between 1 and 100.

2. The player will try to guess the number.

3. After each guess, the computer will give a hint, such as "too high" or "too low".

4. The player will keep guessing until they correctly guess the number.

5. The game will end when the player correctly guesses the number or runs out of attempts (5 attempts).

Guess a number between 1 and 100.
